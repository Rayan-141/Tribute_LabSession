About Rohit

Rohit Sharma is an Indian international cricketer and the captain of the Indian cricket team. Known as the "Hitman" for his explosive batting style, he has set multiple world records in limited-overs cricket. He’s one of the most elegant and consistent performers in the modern era of cricket.

Why I Admire Him

  • World Record: Highest individual score in ODIs – 264 runs.
  • Legendary Performance: 5 centuries in a single World Cup – the most by any player.
  • Leadership: Calm, composed, and inspirational captain.
